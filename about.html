About SHIKARI

Discover our story, our passion for culinary excellence, and the team behind your unforgettable dining experiences

Our Story

Founded in 2025, SHIKARI began as a small family-owned restaurant with a simple mission: to bring people together through exceptional food and warm hospitality. What started as a humble eatery has grown into one of Accra's most celebrated dining destinations.

Our name, SHIKARI, meaning "hunter" in Hindi, reflects our relentless pursuit of the finest ingredients and culinary techniques from around the world. We believe that great food begins with exceptional ingredients, which is why we work directly with local farmers, fishermen, and artisans to source the freshest seasonal produce.

At SHIKARI, we blend traditional cooking methods with innovative approaches to create dishes that are both familiar and exciting. Our menu is a celebration of global flavors with a distinct West African influence, paying homage to our roots while embracing culinary diversity.

Beyond the food, we're committed to creating memorable experiences. From our carefully curated wine selection to our attentive service, every aspect of your visit is designed to delight and inspire.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Sustainability

We're committed to environmentally responsible practices, from sourcing local ingredients to minimizing food waste and reducing our carbon footprint.

Passion

Our team is driven by a genuine love for food and hospitality. This passion shines through in every dish we create and every guest we serve.

Community

We believe in giving back to the community that supports us through local partnerships, charity events, and supporting emerging culinary talent.
